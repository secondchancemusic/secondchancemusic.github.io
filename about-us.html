Hi! we are Second Chance Music! We are an organization that collects old instruments, refurbishes them, and then donates them to local schools. Our organization was started by a middle school student who recognized a need for musical instruments in schools where students have a passion for music, but lack the resources to obtain them. We currently only reside in the northern New Jersey area.
